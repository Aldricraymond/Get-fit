Athletic Strength Training

Strength training is a methodical strategy used by athletes to increase their muscle strength, power, and endurance. It consists of resistance training, like lifting weights, that is customized for each athlete's activity and focuses on improving functional motions, preventing injuries, and improving overall athleticism.

Body Building

Bodybuilding training uses scientific methodologies and resistance exercises to build muscle size, symmetry, and definition. It promotes a mind-muscle link for maximum growth by emphasizing progressive overload, high volume, correct technique, periodization, targeted muscles, recovery, exercise diversity, and nutrient timing.

Powerlifting

Through intense resistance training, powerlifting aims to maximize strength in the squat, bench press, and deadlift. It places a strong emphasis on periodization, recovery, appropriate technique, and increasing overload. Building muscle, increasing range of motion, mobility and maximizing central nervous system (CNS) for peak performance.


General Fitness Training

To increase strength, endurance, and general health, general fitness training incorporates aerobic training, bodyweight exercises, weight training, and high-intensity interval training. Bodyweight exercises improve mobility and balance, while weight training increases bone and muscle density. While HIIT speeds up fitness and weight reduction, cardiovascular training improves heart health and endurance. When combined, they produce a flexible regimen for the best possible physical and mental health.
